Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet, etc.) when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and activities. Cookies can be "session cookies," which are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent cookies," which remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.
